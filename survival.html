Grounded Empyrion-Galactic Survival 7 Days to Die
Grounded is a survival based game where you play as a shrunken teenager. You fight bugs and use grass and weeds to build houses and survive, all while trying to find a way to turn yourself back to normal size. Empyrion is a space/planet based survival game where you can construct your own space stations and spacecraft to survive and collect resources. 7 Days to Die is a zombie survival game where you have to explore and kill zombies for resources to survive and build a base to defend yourself against zombie hordes.
